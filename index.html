Welcome to FundPicker

FundPicker provides a personalized ranking algorithm and comprehensive views from multiple perspectives for investors to efficiently select optimal mutual funds from a large number of fund candidates. Among them, two novel visualizations are proposed: augmented stacked bar chart (in the fund list view) and elastic trend chart (in the fund comparison view). The augmented stacked bar chart provides interpretable fund rankings by revealing both positive and negative factors. The elastic trend chart enables investors flexibly analyze the performance of funds over different periods, which can satisfy different investorsâ€™ needs in their comparison and analysis of mutual funds.
