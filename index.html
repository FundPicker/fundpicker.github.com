Welcome to FundPicker

Mutual funds are one of the most important and popular investment ways for investors to maintain and increase the value of their assets. However, it is challenging to select proper mutual funds and make wise investment decisions due to the large number of mutual funds and the intrinsic data complexity in mutual funds. To address this challenge, we propose a visual analysis pipeline and system to assist investors in effectively selecting proper mutual funds from thousands of fund choices. Through a preference-based ranking algorithm and projection method, we allow investors to browse funds in an orderly manner. We provide six well-coordinated views that help investors analyze and compare the performance of all aspects of the funds they are interested in, so that they can find the right target from thousands of funds. In the fund list view, we propose a novel design to present the results given by the ranking algorithm in an interpretable way. We conducted detailed evaluations, including three case studies, eight in-depth interviews with professional investors, and quantitative experiments for the ranking algorithm to evaluate the usefulness and effectiveness of our system.
